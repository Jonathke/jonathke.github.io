Self Portrait
I asked, and I begged, and I pled:
"Please accept my paper", I said.
Zero accepts,
three hard rejects,
so I went with poems instead.

Talks with famous cryptographer
"Your work has had such a huge impact.
It changed my whole life, in fact!"
She started to glow,
but little did she know,
that I had only glanced at the abstract.

Impostor Syndrome
This talk had me lost at slide three,
so I asked an esteemed attendee.
He tried to explain,
but it was all in vain,
'cause he was even more lost than me.
